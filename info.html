Business Area

Vegetable Seeds

Realizing the unavailability of quality seeds in the Bangladesh market, 2010, we have involved more in introducing Quality seeds together with our international associates. We invested millions & continuing, just to ensure that our farmers are getting quality seeds to produce quality crops for a healthy future.
Now we are working as a trusted backend supplier of Quality Vegetable Seeds for many of well-known retailers across the country. It will be worth to mention that to adapt the future agriculture; we started introducing wide range of innovative products for Vertical Farm, Glass House Farms & Indoor Farms.
As of now almost 40% of our population has got internet access. Considering this, we are introducing our online store for our farmer’s convenient. This will help them to select seeds they require and get those delivered at their doorstep by making payments through mobile banking system.

Farm Equipment

Considering the climate changing issues and off course human health, we have involved ourselves in research, sourcing & delivering of top quality agricultural inputs to produce soilless vegetables crops using less water, less fertilizers & without chemical pesticides. We are working together with world leading horticultural inputs researchers & providers from The Netherlands, Spain & Japan to get the best outcome for the farmers & obviously for the country as a whole.

Plant Care Software - Indoor Farms

Cutting edge technologies enable entrepreneurs to grow fresh crops locally and throughout the year. Based on different hydroponics techniques, our technology includes irrigation, lighting, climate, automation and farm management solutions, and vertical farming equipment as well as agronomic crop growth recipes.
Running a vertical farm may seem tricky; that’s why we are developing application that enables farmers to manage their farm in a couple of clicks. Our Platform contains knowledge sourced from hundreds of farming professionals - agronomists, plant health specialists, engineers, biologists, etc. and lets farmer make smart decisions to achieve greater yields.

Specialized Export Projects

It’s our pleasure to declare that, we are preparing to implement a commercial food production project in different parts of the country with the purpose to export to different countries. For complying with the Safe food guidelines, these particular projects have to follow the GSPP [Good Plant & Production Practice] rules in every phases of its production. We have already entered into specific contract with farmers for contractual production of these crops. We hope, these projects will not only cause good financial revenue for us but also will have a great impact of our economy as a whole.
